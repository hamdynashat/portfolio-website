About Me

From a young age, I've always been fascinated by technology and creativity. Whether it was exploring how things work or imagining new ideas, I found joy in blending logic with imagination. That early passion became the foundation of my journey.

When I joined the Faculty of Computers & Informatics at Tanta University, I finally had the chance to turn this curiosity into a real career path. As a student, I was eager to push beyond theory and start creating. That's when I discovered Flutter, and it completely transformed the way I looked at software development.

What started as experimentation during my second year quickly grew into a deep interest in cross-platform mobile apps—a way to bring ideas to life and share them with the world. Alongside Flutter and Dart, I built skills in UI/UX design, databases, Git/GitHub, testing, and deployment, constantly seeking new challenges to grow.

Beyond coding, I express my creative side through content creation, photography, and storytelling. My vision is clear: to become a recognized programmer and digital creator who inspires, entertains, and adds value to people's lives through technology.
